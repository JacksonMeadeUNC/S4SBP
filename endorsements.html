Our

Endorsements

The Sam Robinson team is so grateful for these organizations' support. We've made sure to reflect their goals for our university in our platform, and we are committed to achieving them once we're in office.

Vegans for Peace at UNC

We are so excited to bring on Vegans for Peace as an official endorser. Their commitment to promoting justice and empowering students through veganism is inspiring. Our platform is committed to expanding access and diversity of food across campus, and hope to have them as a partner.

Carolina Homelessness Prevention Initiative

The work that they do across Orange County and the Carolina Community is a testament to the great possibilities achievable when Tar Heels find commitment to purpose. We hope to have them as a partner in our administration, particularly in implementing our platform proposal to create a Homelessness Prevention Emergency Fund in order to break down barriers to housing.
